About

Inspired by the Olive Grove

At Olivar Design, we are inspired by the olive grove, where every element belongs to a larger intentional system. Our design philosophy is simple: we create places where serenity, privacy, heritage, and greenery come together.

We specialize in premium leisure and recreational architecture, designing high-end chalhets, boutique resorts, private istirahas, and rest houses with a focus on comfort, hospitality, privacy, and connection to nature.

With over 200 projects completed across Saudi Arabia, we bring deep local understanding to every design, ensuring each project is rooted in its environment while maintaining a world-class standard.

Headquartered in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, with an office in Arizona, United States.
